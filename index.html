Accountibility

📊 Transparency & Accountability

I am committed to bringing full transparency to URSA’s financial decisions, including costs, expenditures, scholarships, grants, and all existing funds. Students deserve to clearly understand how their fees are being used and how resources are allocated. I will work to publish regular, easy-to-understand financial reports, ensure open communication, and create opportunities for student input in funding decisions. My goal is to build trust by making URSA’s finances more visible, accountable, and aligned with the real needs of students.
